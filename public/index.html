Common Questions

Everything You Want to Know
Before You Reach Out

Do I need to make repairs before selling? +

Never. We buy homes in any condition — outdated kitchens, roof issues, foundation cracks, full gut jobs. You don't touch a thing.

Is this only for people in financial trouble? +

Not at all. Many sellers are downsizing, relocating, or just don't want the hassle of the MLS. If skipping commissions and showings sounds good to you, we're worth a conversation.

What areas of Georgia do you buy in? +

All of Georgia. We're most active in Cobb County (Mableton, Marietta, Powder Springs), Stone Mountain, and the greater Atlanta metro — but we'll travel anywhere in the state for the right property.

Why not just list with an agent or on Zillow? +

That's always an option and may be right for some people. We're simply another route — one that tends to be faster, simpler, and without the commission fees. Worth a conversation to see which makes more sense for your situation.

How fast can we close? +

As fast as you need. Some sellers close in 7–10 days. Others want 60–90 days to get their next move sorted. We work on your timeline, not ours.

What if I know someone else who wants to sell? +

We'd love to hear from you. Fill out the form and let us know — we'll reach out and take it from there.

What if I'm not ready to sell yet? +

That's completely fine. Submit your property now and we'll have a no-pressure conversation. When you're ready, you'll already know what to expect from us.

Are there any fees or commissions? +

Zero. We are the buyer. No agents involved on your side. You keep every dollar of your agreed sale price.
